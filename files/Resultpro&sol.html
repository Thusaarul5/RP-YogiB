Research Problem & Solution

  • How efficient is the yoga pose classification using machine learning algorithms?
  • How does an accuracy tracker impact a yoga practitioner?
  • How helpful is a 3D visualization feedback generator for a yoga beginner?



The research project aims to suggest an effective and accurate yoga asana based on user requests, recognizing the physical and mental benefits of different yoga asanas and adapting them to meet individual needs. The real-time yoga assistant will assess the user's posture and movement using computer vision and machine learning techniques to suggest areas that need attention. The goal is to improve health and well-being by supporting safer and more effective yoga practice. Technology improvements are crucial for making yoga more effective, personalized, and accessible to a wider range of users, including beginners and those with unique health issues. The real-time yoga assistant will need increased precision, accuracy, and overall efficiency in computer vision to predict human positions during yoga asanas. For beginners, current algorithms and systems often lack the ability to offer individualized and trustworthy feedback. To enable safer and more effective yoga practice, the research challenge necessitates the creation and improvement of computer vision algorithms, user-friendly interfaces, and personalization strategies.
